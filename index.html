ABOUT

Hack the City is inviting 500 students of all skill levels to gather on NYU's campus to create the extraordinary. We'll cover food & travel, bring the best mentors, hold workshops & talks, and provide hardware resources so you can focus on creating the extraordinary.


Our goal as Hack The City is to bring together a community of people from all walks of life. Wether you are a coder, a designer, a business person, alien, or have no idea what you are. We welcome you to our city with open arms and wish the best for all of you.


Welcome to Hack The City's main webpage, and we hope to see you soon!

FAQ

What is Hack The City?

Hack The City is a purely student organized hackathon. Made by students, for students, with the goal of bringing together a bigger and brighter hacking community.

What should I bring?

Laptop, chargers for any electronic devices you plan on bringing. Student ID, any necessary toiletries, and if you so desire, a change of clothes and a sleeping bag.

How can I get there

We might be doing travel reimbursement. Even we don't know yet! But we most definitely will be (maybe?) sending a bus to a college near you! (unless you're in bumfuck nowhere. in which case ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ )

When will it be taking place?

Hack The City is scheduled for April 9th and 10th. However if this gets changed, we will let you know through this website

I am uber n00b

We encourage all to participate, no matter the skill level. Hackathons are a place to learn new things, meet new friends, and make mistakes.

Will food be provided?

Yes, we will be providing lunch and dinner for Saturday, and then breakfast and lunch on Sunday. Also we will have snacks prepared for hackers.

How many people per team?

Four.

What if I don't have a team/idea?

Totally fine! Hackathons are great places to meet people, and we'll be holding a speed dating sesh where you can meet new people, find that special someone, and get right to building!

What should I not bring?

A closed mind? But for real. Guns, drugs, and alcohol are a no go.

What's your favorite color?

As of right now, it's orange. Is that all? I'm leaving.
