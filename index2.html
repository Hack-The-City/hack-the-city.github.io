ABOUT

Hack the City is inviting 500 students of all skill levels to gather on NYU's campus to create the extraordinary. We'll cover food & travel, bring the best mentors, and provide hardware resources so you can focus on making friends, learning something new, and having fun.

FAQ

What is Hack The City?

Hack The City is a purely student organized hackathon made by student hackers, for student hackers with the goal of bringing together a bigger and brighter community in NYC

What should I bring?

Laptop, charges for any electroonic! devices you plan on bringing, studentID, any necessary toiletries, if you so desire, a change of clothes.

How can I get there

We might be doing travel reimbursement. Even we don't know yet! But we most definitely will be (maybe?) sending a bus to a college near you! (unless you're in bumfuck nowhere. in which case ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ )

When will it be taking place?

The ETA of Hack The City will be April 9th and 10th. However if this gets changed, we will let you know through this website

I am uber n00b

We encourage all to participate, no matter the skill level. Hackathons are places to learn new things, and wether you may be the uberist n00berist person out there, we really hope you come.
